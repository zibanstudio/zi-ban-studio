About

Hi, I'm Zi Ban

I’m a Photography and Media student based in California, working across photography, visual design, and web-based presentation. My work focuses on image-making, layout, and digital structure. I’m interested in how a website can function as part of a visual identity rather than simply a place to store content. I’m drawn to work that feels restrained, atmospheric, and precise, where typography, pacing, and visual rhythm matter as much as the images themselves.
